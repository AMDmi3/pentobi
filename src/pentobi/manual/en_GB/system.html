System Requirements

Minimum: 1 GB RAM, 1 GHz CPU
Recommended for playing level 8: 2 GB RAM, dual-core 2 GHz CPU

Pentobi can be used on systems that do not meet the minimum requirements if only the lower playing levels are used. However the highest playing level can be very slow on those systems (if the CPU is too slow) or have a reduced playing strength (if there is not enough memory).
